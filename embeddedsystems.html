Electronic Game Arduino

This was a solo project for Embedded Systems at University of Wisconsin - Whitewater.

The project was to create a device using an Arduino.

I created a mini handheld electronic game device. The device has four colored buttons with four matching LED lights, as well as a buzzer to make noise.

The device has three modes:

  • Simon Says mode, where the player memorizes a series of increasingly long inputs
  • Reaction Test mode, where the player must press the correct button within a progressively shortening window of time
  • Free Play mode, where the play may press any button to light up the corresponding light and make the corresponding sound
