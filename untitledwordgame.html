Untitled Word Game

Untitled Word Game was a capstone project I created at University of Wisconsin - Whitewater.

It is a local multiplayer versus game where two players take turns spelling words using letters, similar to Scrabble or Boggle.

The longer a word is, the more damage it does to the opponent. When one player runs out of health, they lose, and the other player wins.

The game was made in Unity and programmed in C#. This was a solo project that I worked on singlehandedly.

After creating this project for my capstone class, I decided to continue working on it afterwards with the intent of releasing it as a full-fledged game.
